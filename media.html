About Neorons (150 words)

Neorons is a youth-led social-impact organization advancing science, technology, and inclusion for young people across India. Founded in 2025, Neorons designs and fully funds education programmes in partnership with district education departments, government schools, and community organizations.

Programmes span three areas: STEM for Social Good (hackathons, science fairs, and school wellbeing programmes), Inclusion and Ability (sport and technology programmes for students with disabilities), and AI and Education (teacher training and student AI literacy). Since launch, Neorons has reached over 1,200 students across eight districts in five states and Delhi, trained 140 educators in AI-supported teaching, and supported 84 blind and low-vision athletes in competitive sport. Every programme is free for participants.
