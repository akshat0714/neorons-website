Who we are

Every young person, regardless of geography, ability, or circumstance, deserves the tools of science and the dignity of opportunity.

Neorons works alongside schools, educators, and local communities to deliver programmes that are rigorous, inclusive, and built to last: hackathons that put student ingenuity to work on public-health challenges, championships that celebrate the athleticism of blind girls, and classrooms learning to use artificial intelligence well. Our work is united by a single idea: potential is everywhere; opportunity must be too.

We deliver every programme in collaboration with district education departments, government schools, and community organizations.

Our people

Founded and led by students.

Neorons is youth-led. Our team is made up of students and recent graduates, supported by an advisory circle of educators, clinicians, and engineers who review every programme we run.

We are grateful to our advisory circle of practising psychiatrists, psychologists, and educators, and to everyone who works alongside us to keep our programmes safe and rigorous.

What we do

Technology and opportunity as instruments of inclusion.

Every programme we run is an expression of one idea: science and technology should serve everyone. Three areas, one mission.

STEM for Social Good

Hackathons, science fairs, and school wellbeing programmes that channel student talent toward real public challenges, from suicide prevention and peer support to assistive technology.

Inclusion & Ability

Funding and organizing sport and technology programmes for students with disabilities. Inclusion is not charity; it is infrastructure.

AI & Education

Training teachers and equipping classrooms to use artificial intelligence responsibly, so that no district is left behind in the decade ahead.

How we count

"Students reached" means unique registered participants across all programmes. We do not count family attendees, community visitors, or online viewers. Our total of 1,200+ is conservative; the actual number of people who experienced our events is higher.
